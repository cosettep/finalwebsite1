Education

I graduated from Los Alamos High School in 2010 and continued my education at University of New Mexico Los Alamos and Albuquerque. I first studied International Affairs and took a break as I found myself suddenly in a finance career. I went back to school a few years ago and studied Architecture. Due to COVID and being a new mom, I had to pause my higher education to stay home and care for my family. I have returned Fall 2022 and I am studying Intercultural Communications. I absolutely love this program and I am even more ecstatic that I can complete this degree online so I can continue to care for my family. I have worked very hard this semester and I have given myself a personal goal of making the Dean’s List. I am really excited to see how that turns out. I have been working on 15 credits this semester and that is not easy while handling a wild toddler. I am very pleased and proud with how much I have learned this semester and I have already been able to apply my new skills to other courses and my personal life. My anticipated graduation year is 2024, but I am a junior at UNM. This is in part due to the switch of major. I need to pick a minor for my major and I am planning on choosing Sociology. I have enjoyed my sociology course this semester, but I also feel that this best compliments the Intercultural Communications major.
