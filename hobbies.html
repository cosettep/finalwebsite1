Hobbies

Flavors included chocolate chip stuffed with Nutella, red velvet stuffed with cream cheese frosting, and a German chocolate cake stuffed with coconut pecan frosting. I really enjoy playing around with classic recipes and making them a different bakery item such as the giant gourmet cake cookies. I do miss baking and cannot wait to get back to it for winter break.
