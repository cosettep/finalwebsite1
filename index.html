Welcome to my website

My name is Cosette and I am a student at the University of New Mexico. I am studying Intercultural Communication. I love the communication program and find it highly interesting while pushing my boundaries of cultural understanding. This is my first semester returning to the University of New Mexico. I previously studied International Affairs and Architecture. Both of these programs I loved as well. I chose my major as it is a passion of mine to work with different cultures and societies as well as the program allowing me to study online. Being a stay at home mother makes it very difficult to attend in person schooling, but I am happy UNM provides many online options for learning. I have other passions such as baking and working with vulnerable populations. I have 15 years of volunteer work for homeless shelters and the Special Olympics. Some more about me, I have worked in finance for several years, but found no passion behind it and decided to quit and go back to school. My resume is attached to this site and I created it in my design class this semester. You will also find a portfolio to show some of my creative work from this semester, an education page, a contact page, and a professional goals page. I hope you enjoy my website and find all needed information about me and my background. This semester has been very difficult, but also very rewarding and I look forward to continuing my education with the University of New.
